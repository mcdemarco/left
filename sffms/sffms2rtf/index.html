rtf converter

This is a beta version of a forthcoming sffms to rtf converter. Upload a story file (e.g., oveja.tex) and it will returned to you in Rich Text Format. Since this is a beta, you should edit the output in a word processor before emailing it to anyone.

Text that the converter could not interpret will be marked in red. The output file will have the extension .rtf and may be downloaded automatically, depending on your browser. No copy of your story is saved on the server.
