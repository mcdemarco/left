DotScap is a proofing format that lays out a Twine story as a Scapple mind map. However, to go in the other direction---from a Scapple file to a Twine story---requires more than just a story format. This page will do the conversion for you, entirely in your browser. (When you "upload" your file, nothing is sent to the server.)

What do you end up with? Well, a very simple story file with passages in the same positions they were in in Scapple, with the names and links they had there. A somewhat random passage may be chosen to be your start passage.
